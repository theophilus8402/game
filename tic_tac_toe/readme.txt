
Game: Tic-Tac-Toe

Board: a 3x3 grid
Pieces: X's and O's
Rules:
  * Players take turns placing a piece on the board
  * X's start first
  * A player may not place a piece on an already occupied spot
  * A piece may not be moved once it's been placed
  * A player wins when they get three of their pieces in a row, col, or diag
  * The game will end either when a player wins or all the spots are filled
